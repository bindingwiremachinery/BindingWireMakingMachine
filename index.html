About Us

Home / About Us

Pioneering Industrial Manufacturing Since Inception

Binding Wire Making Machine is a premier engineering corporation based in Rajkot, Gujarat. We specialize in the design, development, and manufacturing of heavy-duty machinery for the wire and nail industry.

Our core philosophy revolves around delivering robust, high-yielding, and cost-effective production solutions. Over the years, we have mastered the metallurgy and kinematics required to produce machines that run 24/7 with minimal maintenance. Our IN-series nail making machines and wire drawing plants are recognized globally for their precision and longevity.

"Our mission is to empower global manufacturers with unyielding machinery that scales their production seamlessly and profitably."
  • Quality Assurance: Multi-stage inspection process.
  • R&D Focus: Continuous improvement in machine efficiency.
  • After-Sales Support: Global spare parts availability and technical guidance.
